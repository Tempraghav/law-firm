Why to Put Trust Your Trust In KMS Chambers - Advocates and Solicitors

Kumkum Mandhanya Saboo is a dedicated advocate committed to delivering exceptional legal services tailored to her clients’ unique needs. With a dynamic legal career spanning multiple jurisdictions, including Delhi, Bhopal, and now Jaipur, Kumkum specializes in family and matrimonial law, civil and criminal litigation, and commercial arbitration. Her personalized approach and unwavering focus on achieving positive outcomes have earned her the trust and respect of her clients. Kumkum’s practice areas include family law, matrimonial law, child custody, property disputes, contractual defaults, injunctions, transfer of petitions, POSH law, arbitration, and commercial matters. She has successfully represented clients in the Supreme Court, various High Courts, as well as District and Sessions Courts, handling complex and high-stakes legal matters with precision and care. Her accolades include recognition for Best Written Submission Overall and Best Written Submission (Complainant) at the 18th John H. Jackson International Moot Court Competition (South & West Asia Regional Round) in 2020 and a win at the prestigious Pendakanti Venkatasubbaiah Memorial National Moot Court Competition, Hyderabad, in 2018. Kumkum was also honored as ‘Moot Mentor - 2021’ for guiding teams in national and international moot court competitions and served as a Panel Judge for the 6th Zeal Moot Court Competition at SLSH in 2022. With a proven track record and a results-driven mindset, Kumkum Mandhanya Saboo is committed to providing strategic, effective, and comprehensive legal counsel. If you’re seeking a reliable and results-oriented advocate, you can trust her expertise and dedication to guide you through your legal journey.
